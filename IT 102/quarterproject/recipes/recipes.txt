Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:
> Chicken
> Eggs
> Rice, day old rice works best!
> Neutral Oil
> Seasonings of choice
> Soy Sauce
> Garlic Cloves

Directions:

---Eggs---
> Heat a pan to medium-low
> Crack desired amount of eggs into bowl and whisk until yolks are scrambled
> Once pan is heated to medium-low add in neutral oil and egg
> Scramble eggs and cook till done. Set aside. 
---Chicken---
> Prepare the chicken for cooking by coating with desired seasonings.
> Heat up a pan with available neutral oil, cook chicken till cooked.
> Cut chicken into bite sized pieces. 
---Fried rice---
> Dice desired amount of garlic cloves.
> Heat pan to medium with available neutral oil, and add in diced garlic.
> Once garlic is aromatic, add in cooked rice and mix, breaking apart any pieces of rice stuck together. 
> Add in 2 tbps of soy sauce and mix thoroughly coating rice in soy sauce.
> Add in cooked egg and chicken.
> Turn off heat and plate.

Creamy Spinach Pasta

Ingredients:
> Spinach, fresh or frozen
> 1/2 cup Heavy cream OR Milk
> Seasonings of choice (rec: garlic powder, onion powder)
> Pasta
> Parmesan Cheese
> Pinch of salt
> Neutral Oil

Directions:

---Pasta--- 
> Bring a pot of water to boil
> Salt to taste once the water comes to a boil
> Add in available pasta type, and cook according to instructions to al dente
> Save 1 cup of pasta water, and strain pasta once pasta is cooked to al dente and strain pasta noodles under cool water to prevent further cooking. Set pasta aside.

---Pasta Sauce---
> Heat up pot used to boil pasta noodles to medium-low heat
> Add in 1 half cup of cream/milk and bring to a simmer.
> Once cream is at a simmer, drop the heat to low, mix in pasta 1/4 cup of pasta water and add in desired amount of parmesan cheese and the spinach.
> Season sauce with your seasonings of choice.
> Continue to heat sauce until sauce reaches desired thickness.

---Putting it all together---
> Add pasta noodles to sauce, and serve once fully coated. Top with additional parmesan cheese and enjoy!

Creamy Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:
> Chicken
> 1/2 Heavy cream OR Milk
> Seasonings of choice (rec: garlic powder, onion powder)
> Pasta
> Parmesan Cheese
> Pinch of salt
> Neutral Oil


Directions:

---Pasta--- 
> Bring a pot of water to boil
> Salt to taste once the water comes to a boil
> Add in available pasta type, and cook according to instructions to al dente
> Save 1 cup of pasta water, and strain pasta once pasta is cooked to al dente and strain pasta noodles under cool water to prevent further cooking. Set pasta aside.
---Chicken--- 
> While pasta cooks, prepare the chicken for cooking by coating with desired seasonings.
> Heat up a pan with available neutral oil, cook chicken till fully cooked.
> Let chicken cool and cut into bite-sized pieces.
---Pasta Sauce---
> Heat up pot used to boil pasta noodles to medium-low heat
> Add in 1 half cup of cream/milk and bring to a simmer.
> Once cream is at a simmer, drop the heat to low, mix in pasta 1/4 cup of pasta water and add in desire amount of parmesan cheese.
> Add same seasonings as you did to the chicken to the sauce.
> Continue to heat sauce until sauce reaches desired thickness.
---Putting it all together---
> Add pasta noodles to sauce, and serve once fully coated. Top with chicken, optional additional cheese and enjoy!
